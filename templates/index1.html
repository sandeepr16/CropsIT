About Us

Farmers with low crop yield need instant soil quality report to cultivate suitable crops to increase crop production. Farmers often face certain issues since they are not used to the advancing technology. Also, soil testing laboratories take time to give the analysis report. This process is not instantaneous and is ineffective for day to day analysis. Our Solution is an easily operable kiosk which senses the soil nutrient components and analyses the values obtained to provide a detailed report, along with crop suggestions suitable for the soil, instantaneously through a message service.The salient features of the model are Instantaneous analysis, suitable crops and estimated yield, suitable fertilizer to maintain fertility and effective communication through messages. The device primarily measures the amount of macro nutrients present in the soil, collects the information regarding various parameters that effect the soil quality when required, analyses the collected information using appropriate data science techniques, suggests the farmer with the crop varieties suitable for that land, market demand is also taken into consideration while suggesting, provides information regarding the right fertilizer that is to be used, conveys the required data to the farmer using a message service platform.
